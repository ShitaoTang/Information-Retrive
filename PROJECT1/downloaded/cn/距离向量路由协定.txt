					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content"><div class="mw-content-ltr mw-parser-output" lang="zh-Hans-CN" dir="ltr"><div id="noteTA-2bfcebc4" class="noteTA"><div class="noteTA-group"><div data-noteta-group-source="module" data-noteta-group="Telcom"></div></div></div>
<p><b>距离向量路由协议</b>（英语：<span lang="en">distance-vector routing protocol</span>），为<a href="/wiki/%E8%B7%AF%E7%94%B1%E5%8D%94%E5%AE%9A" class="mw-redirect" title="路由协议">路由协议</a>中的两大分类之一，这类协议采用距离向量（distance-vector，缩写为DV）算法来决定报文交换的路径。包括<a href="/wiki/%E8%B4%9D%E5%B0%94%E6%9B%BC-%E7%A6%8F%E7%89%B9%E7%AE%97%E6%B3%95" title="贝尔曼-福特算法">贝尔曼-福特算法</a>，<span class="ilh-all" data-orig-title="Ford–Fulkerson algorithm" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-foreign-title="Ford–Fulkerson algorithm"><span class="ilh-page"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ford%E2%80%93Fulkerson_algorithm&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Ford–Fulkerson algorithm（页面不存在）">Ford–Fulkerson algorithm</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ford%E2%80%93Fulkerson_algorithm" class="extiw" title="en:Ford–Fulkerson algorithm"><span lang="en" dir="auto">Ford–Fulkerson algorithm</span></a></span>）</span></span>与<span class="ilh-all" data-orig-title="DUAL FSM" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-foreign-title="Diffusing update algorithm"><span class="ilh-page"><a href="/w/index.php?title=DUAL_FSM&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="DUAL FSM（页面不存在）">DUAL FSM</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diffusing_update_algorithm" class="extiw" title="en:Diffusing update algorithm"><span lang="en" dir="auto">Diffusing update algorithm</span></a></span>）</span></span>等算法，都被归类于距离向量算法中。
</p><p>这类协议包括<a href="/wiki/%E8%B7%AF%E7%94%B1%E4%BF%A1%E6%81%AF%E5%8D%8F%E8%AE%AE" title="路由信息协议">路由信息协议</a>（RIP）及<a href="/wiki/%E5%86%85%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%91%E5%85%B3%E5%8D%8F%E8%AE%AE" title="内部网关协议">内部网关协议</a>（IGP）等。在这类协议中，<a href="/wiki/%E8%B7%AF%E7%94%B1%E5%99%A8" title="路由器">路由器</a>需要周期性与相邻的路由器交换更新通告（routing updates），动态建立路由表，以决定最短路径。
</p>
