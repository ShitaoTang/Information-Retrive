					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content"><div class="mw-content-ltr mw-parser-output" lang="zh-Hans-CN" dir="ltr"><p><b>仪表盘</b>，通常会是一块<a href="/wiki/%E5%B9%B3%E6%9D%BF" class="mw-redirect" title="平板">平板</a>，作用是装上<a href="/wiki/%E4%BB%AA%E8%A1%A8" class="mw-redirect mw-disambig" title="仪表">仪表</a>或有关的装置，如<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%AD%A6%E5%A0%B1%E7%87%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="警报灯（页面不存在）">警报灯</a>，提供<a href="/wiki/%E4%BD%BF%E7%94%A8%E8%80%85" class="mw-redirect" title="使用者">使用者</a><a href="/wiki/%E8%B3%87%E8%A8%8A" class="mw-redirect" title="资讯">资讯</a>或<a href="/wiki/%E8%AD%A6%E5%8F%B7" class="mw-disambig" title="警号">警号</a>。仪表盘更可装上照明系统，或接上控制台。
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span id=".E6.B1.BD.E8.BB.8A.E5.84.80.E8.A1.A8.E7.9B.A4"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="汽車儀表盤">汽车仪表盘</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="编辑章节：汽车仪表盘"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">主条目：<a href="/wiki/%E6%B1%BD%E8%BB%8A%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8" title="汽车仪表">汽车仪表</a></div>
<p>汽车仪表盘是汽车专用的仪表盘。汽车仪表盘通常会装有以下仪表。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E8.BD.A6.E9.80.9F.E9.87.8C.E7.A8.8B.E5.84.80.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="车速里程儀表">车速里程仪表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="编辑章节：车速里程仪表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%BD%A6%E9%80%9F%E9%87%8C%E7%A8%8B%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="车速里程表（页面不存在）">车速里程仪表</a>由两个仪表组成：车速表和里程表。这些主要<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E9%A1%AF%E7%A4%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="显示（页面不存在）">显示</a>车速和驾驶里程。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E8.BD.AC.E9.80.9F.E5.84.80.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="转速儀表">转速仪表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="编辑章节：转速仪表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E8%BD%AC%E9%80%9F%E8%A1%A8" class="mw-redirect" title="转速表">转速仪表</a>主要显示发动机的转速。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E6.9C.BA.E6.B2.B9.E5.8E.8B.E5.8A.9B.E5.84.80.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="机油压力儀表">机油压力仪表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="编辑章节：机油压力仪表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%9C%BA%E6%B2%B9%E5%A3%93%E5%8A%9B%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="机油压力表（页面不存在）">机油压力仪表</a>主要显示<a href="/wiki/%E6%A9%9F%E6%B2%B9" title="机油">机油</a>所承受的<a href="/wiki/%E5%8E%8B%E5%8A%9B" class="mw-redirect" title="压力">压力</a>。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E6.B0.B4.E6.B8.A9.E5.84.80.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="水温儀表">水温仪表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="编辑章节：水温仪表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B4%E6%B8%A9%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="水温表（页面不存在）">水温仪表</a>主要显示汽车<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%86%B7%E5%8D%BB%E6%B0%B4%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="冷却水道（页面不存在）">冷却水道</a>中的<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B4%E6%B8%A9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="水温（页面不存在）">水温</a>。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E7.87.83.E6.B2.B9.E5.84.80.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="燃油儀表">燃油仪表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="编辑章节：燃油仪表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E7%87%83%E6%B2%B9%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="燃油表（页面不存在）">燃油仪表</a>主要显示<a href="/wiki/%E7%87%83%E6%B2%B9" class="mw-redirect" title="燃油">燃油</a>剩余量。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E6.8C.87.E7.A4.BA.E5.92.8C.E8.AD.A6.E6.8A.A5.E7.87.88"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="指示和警报燈">指示和警报灯</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="编辑章节：指示和警报灯"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>汽车仪表盘通常会有一些<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%8C%87%E7%A4%BA%E7%81%AF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="指示灯（页面不存在）">指示</a>和警报灯，各有功用。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E9.A3.9B.E8.A1.8C.E5.84.80.E9.8C.B6"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="飛行儀錶">飞行仪表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="编辑章节：飞行仪表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">主条目：<a href="/wiki/%E9%A3%9E%E8%A1%8C%E4%BB%AA%E8%A1%A8" class="mw-redirect" title="飞行仪表">飞行仪表</a></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E9%A3%9B%E8%A1%8C" title="飞行">飞行</a>仪表盘上通常设有六种基本仪表，分别是<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E7%A9%BA%E9%80%9F%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="空速表（页面不存在）">空速表</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E5%A7%BF%E6%85%8B%E5%84%80" class="mw-redirect" title="姿态仪">姿态仪</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E9%AB%98%E5%BA%A6%E8%A1%A8" class="mw-redirect" title="高度表">高度表</a>、<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%BD%89%E5%BD%8E%E5%8D%94%E8%AA%BF%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="转弯协调表（页面不存在）">转弯协调表</a>、<a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%AA%E5%90%91%E6%8C%87%E7%A4%BA%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="航向指示表（页面不存在）">航向指示表</a>及<a href="/wiki/%E5%9E%82%E7%9B%B4%E9%80%9F%E7%8E%87%E8%A1%A8" class="mw-redirect" title="垂直速率表">垂直速率表</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id=".E9.AB.98.E5.BA.A6.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="高度表">高度表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="编辑章节：高度表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>高度表主要显示<a href="/wiki/%E9%A3%9B%E6%A9%9F" title="飞机">飞机</a>高度的资讯。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E7.A9.BA.E9.80.9F.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="空速表">空速表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="编辑章节：空速表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>空速表主要功效为测量<a href="/wiki/%E8%A1%9D%E5%A3%93" title="冲压">冲压</a><a href="/wiki/%E7%A9%BA%E6%B0%A3" class="mw-redirect" title="空气">空气</a>的<a href="/wiki/%E5%A3%93%E5%8A%9B" class="mw-redirect" title="压力">压力</a>。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E5.9E.82.E7.9B.B4.E9.80.9F.E7.8E.87.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="垂直速率表">垂直速率表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="编辑章节：垂直速率表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>垂直速率表主要功效为显示<a href="/wiki/%E9%A3%9B%E6%A9%9F" title="飞机">飞机</a>爬升及下降的<a href="/wiki/%E9%80%9F%E7%8E%87" title="速率">速率</a>。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E5.A7.BF.E6.85.8B.E5.84.80"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="姿態儀">姿态仪</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="编辑章节：姿态仪"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>姿态仪主要显示飞机相对于地平线的姿态。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E8.88.AA.E5.90.91.E6.8C.87.E7.A4.BA.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="航向指示表">航向指示表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="编辑章节：航向指示表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>航向指示表主要显示出飞机机头的磁方位。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E8.BD.89.E5.BD.8E.E5.8D.94.E8.AA.BF.E8.A1.A8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="轉彎協調表">转弯协调表</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="编辑章节：转弯协调表"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>转弯协调表主要指示转弯的方向及飞机转弯的速率。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E5.85.B6.E4.BB.96"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="其他">其他</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E8%A1%A8%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="编辑章节：其他"><span>编辑</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>于很多机器、工具中，也设有仪表盘。例如<a href="/wiki/%E8%88%B9" title="船">船</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E9%9B%BB%E5%AD%90%E8%A8%88%E7%AE%97%E6%A9%9F" class="mw-redirect" title="电子计算机">电子计算机</a>等，而其中的仪表亦会相异。
</p>
