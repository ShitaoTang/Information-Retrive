 network working group alexander a mckenzie rfc bbn net nic january imp tip preventive maintenance schedule this note presents the current imp tip preventive maintenance schedule preventive maintenance is scheduled on a monthly basis and a given machine is normally scheduled at the same time each month wherever possible the time is chosen to coincide with the pm time for the host s at the site it is sometimes necessary to reschedule pm s because of network connectivity considerations or for other reasons whenever such rescheduling occurs we will announce the fact as soon as possible via two mechanisms the tip news and a journal note not an rfc to technical liaisons any permanent changes to the schedule will be announced via the rfc mechanism all times show below are eastern time first monday of each month bbn imp first tuesday bbn tip ucsb first wednesday carnegie sri xerox first thursday saac utah second monday mit noaa second tuesday mitre usc second wednesday cca stanford radc mckenzie page rfc imp tip preventive maintenance schedule january second thursday belvoir ucsd patrick third monday harvard ucla third tuesday rand sdc third wednesday illinois arpa ames imp third thursday etac fnwc fourth monday case hawaii fourth tuesday nbs lbl fourth wednesday lincoln ames tip fourth thursday gwc isi aberdeen this rfc was put into machine readable form for entry into the online rfc archives by alex mckenzie with support from gte formerly bbn corp mckenzie page
